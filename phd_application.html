Available PhD Positions

Currently, I have 1-2 available PhD positions for this year (2022) and next year (2023).

Research Topics

Below I list some of my broad research topics which I expect prospective students will work on under my supervision. If you are interested in these topics or have any other research proposals, don't hesitate to contact me and we can discuss further 😄.

  • Label and Data Efficient Deep Learning
  • Responsible and Robust AI
  • Representation Learning

When working on these topics, students may need to use machine learning techniques such as self-supervised learning, generative models, adversarial learning, variational inference, causal inference, disentanglement. Therefore, it will be a plus if prospective students have good theoretical knowledge about and/or practical experience with these techniques.

Documents to be sent

When you contact me, please send me the following documents via email:

  • A comprehensive resume (CV)
  • Theses
  • Academic transcripts
  • Published papers and scientific reports in English (if any)
  • A research proposal specifying at least three reseach problems/challenges you aim to address during your PhD

The last one (research proposal) is important and should be prepared carefully. A good research proposal will make the candidate stand out from others.

Selection Criteria

  • Research inclination
  • Research experience
  • Academic recognition
  • Good mathematical background: Maths is important for high-quality research in ML & AI as it stands behind most advances in the field. Some branches of maths commonly used in ML & AI research include statistics, optimization, algebra, calculus. It would be a great advantage if students could demonstrate their deep understanding of these topic during interview.
  • Good programming skills: While a good mathematical background enables students to design correct methods, good programming skills enable students to implement their methods effectively and efficiently, which increases their productivity.
  • Competent English: The minimum level for competent English is the Academic IELTS overall score of at least 6.5 with no individual component scores less than 6.0. For more details about this requirement and equivalent scores for other kinds of English test such as PTE Academic or TOEFL, please refer to the table in Part C, Section 5 from this link. Please note that a student could be interviewed without meeting this requirement, but this requirement is compulsory for the student to be granted a PhD scholarship.
