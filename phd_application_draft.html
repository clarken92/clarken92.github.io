Research Topics

Below I list some of my broad research topics which I expect prospective students will work on under my supervision. If you are interested in these topics or have any other research proposals, don't hesitate to contact me and we can discuss further 😄.

  • Label and Data Efficient Deep Learning
  • Responsible and Robust AI
  • Representation Learning

When working on these topics, students may need to use machine learning techniques such as self-supervised learning, generative models, adversarial learning, variational inference, causal inference, disentanglement. Therefore, it will be a plus if prospective students have good theoretical knowledge about and/or practical experience with these techniques.

Documents to be sent

When you contact me, please send me the following documents via email:

  • A comprehensive resume (CV)
  • Theses
  • Academic transcripts
  • Published papers and scientific reports in English (if any)
  • A globally accepted English language certificate (e.g., IELTS, TOEFL, PTE)
  • A research proposal specifying at least three reseach problems/challenges you aim to address during your PhD

The last one (research proposal) is important and should be prepared carefully. A good research proposal will make the candidate stand out from others. Some students may feel reluctant to write a research proposal because it seems hard and boring but they may not be aware that their upcoming PhD could be ten times harder and more boring than the research proposal they've just written. So I hope you would take it easily but not lightly as a small challenge to yourself to see whether you are suitable for research or not.
